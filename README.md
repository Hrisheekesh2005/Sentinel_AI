# 🛡️ Sentinel AI: Agentic Digital Safety Shield

**Sentinel AI** is an advanced Agentic AI system designed to combat the surge of digital fraud (UPI/KYC scams) and viral misinformation in the Indian ecosystem. Unlike traditional filters that merely classify text, Sentinel AI acts as an **Investigative Agent**—perceiving threats, reasoning through evidence, unmasking hidden infrastructure, and executing proactive defense strategies to protect users.

---

## 🎯 The Problem
Digital literacy is struggling to keep pace with rapid digitization. Users face two critical threats:
1. **Financial Scams:** Sophisticated UPI, KYC, and "Electricity Bill" scams that use panic and impersonation.
2. **Misinformation:** Viral health hoaxes and fake government schemes that create social unrest and financial loss.

## 🤖 The "Agentic" Innovation
Sentinel AI is not just a machine learning model; it is an **Autonomous Safety Agent**:
*   **Perception:** It "listens" to voice notes (Vishing) and "reads" text patterns.
*   **Investigation:** It autonomously follows shortened links (bit.ly/tinyurl) using its **URL Unmasking Tool** to find the real destination before the user clicks.
*   **Reasoning:** It uses **Chain-of-Thought (CoT)** reasoning, visualized via an "Internal Monologue," to cross-reference threats against ML patterns, semantic memory, and logic rules.
*   **Action:** It executes defense tasks—generating legal PDF reports, drafting emails to authorities, and alerting community groups via WhatsApp.

---

## 🧠 Triple-Layer Hybrid Brain
The system synthesizes three distinct layers of intelligence for maximum accuracy:
1.  **ML Intent Layer:** A Logistic Regression model (v2.4) trained on Indian scam datasets to detect fraudulent "vibes."
2.  **Semantic Memory:** Uses **Vector Cosine Similarity** to match messages against a database of known "scam blueprints" (e.g., KBC Lottery, FedEx Customs).
3.  **Heuristic Logic Engine:** A deep ruleset that identifies hard evidence (UPI IDs, Phone numbers) and "Legitimacy Markers" (available balances) to eliminate false positives.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **🎙️ Vishing Detector:** Integrated Web Speech API to analyze suspicious phone calls or voice notes in real-time.
- **🌐 Autonomous URL Unmasker:** Proactively follows redirect chains to reveal hidden phishing domains.
- **📄 Dynamic Template Engine:** Autonomously writes a tailored legal report based on the specific category of crime detected.
- **🔍 Search for Truth:** An integrated bridge to global fact-checkers for verifying viral misinformation.
- **🔊 AI Voice Advice:** Spoken safety recommendations specifically designed for elderly and non-technical users.
